Change Request System

How do I submit a change request? Click on the “New Request Form” button, fill in the details and click “Submit”. Your request will be sent for review.


How can I track the status of my request? Go to “View Requests” to see the status of your request, such as Submitted, In Review, or Approved.


Can I edit my request after submitting it? No, you need to contact an admin to make any changes.


Can I see all requests, including those not submitted by me? No, only admins and approvers can see all requests.


Who can change the status of a request? Only admins and approvers can change the status of a request.


What should I do if I’m having trouble with the system? Visit the “help” page or contact support at support@changerequestsystem.com
